My Life

In this page, I will tell, show my life.
Lived over my 17 years.

Steps:

1

Childhood

I was born in Bishkek on November 1, 2002. My mother is a doctor, therapist. Dad is a teacher, electrician. There are 2 older brothers. The youngest in the family. After graduating from Kindergarten, I left for Russia at 6 year.

2

Middle School

I studied in secondary school  in Russia. I studied 4 years in a music school, also 6 years in basketball. School years for me were the best years of my life. Because I met a lot of good people and learned from life to take only the best.

3

Lyceum

After 6th grade, my parents wanted to give me to the Lyceum. They themselves continued to live in Russia, but they left me with my older brother. I studied at the Tokmok Women's Lyceum for 3.5 years. Later I transferred to Bishkek Lyceum Aichurok. At the moment I'm studying there.
